KANLUNGAN

about this.

kanlungan is a safe space "shelter" dedicated for people who might be lost in their pathway of life. adorned in charming digital artwork and led by the fictional shapeshifting cat, Miskonasia, the website holds several little ephemeras in the daily — helpful quotes for the day with an originally written poem in the side, a post-it note station as fleeting as you allow it to be, and a bit of an "achievements" section dictated by the amount of days you log in.

pace your allusions.

perhaps a flick of a coin cannot pave your fate; destiny is an ink of your quill. though so, a word of advice by the daylight surely would suffice to taint your life’s magnum opus of a canvas a bit more iridescent in colour. Miskonasia drops by on the daily with love letters of the heart spurred alike a warm cup of cocoa.

(a page where you receive a quote every day with a sprinkle of a short poem. come back the next, and maybe you’ll get a little sweet treat of an award once on specific dates...)

showers of rain may grace the roots of where one flourished upon the golden star, but to never feel the glint of it roll down your cheek, to not have known who the sun rises for are the elysian fields in a gilded cage.

carve your epithet.

do you yearn to speak aloud of the swooping thoughts of your affinities? perhaps the will of brevity sews your lips into silence — the childish fluster of musing such encomiums even towards the mirror a sight and sound unheard of to you. Miskonasia’s space allows of the love that cannot be named: from a hallway limerence to a simple adoration towards a vitruvian figure you dare not approach.

(a safe space to compliment people either you’re close with or you’ve seen casually when you can’t do it because you’re too shy: completely anonymous. the message will only stay there until you refresh.)

art, poems, lore creds to elisha e.

mold your play.

it is no clandestine an enigma to be held under lock and key the depth of dedication to wreathe such a safe space with letterings of your reflection’s virtuoso. as such, if you’ll allow, Miskonasia would hesitate no less to pull the curtains before the begetters of the Kanlungan for a moment’s relief.
